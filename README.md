# 📊 AI Job Market Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze global AI job market trends. It provides insights into hiring demand, salary distribution, required skills, preferred tools, company sizes, and employment patterns.

The dashboard is divided into two pages:

- Executive Dashboard – High-level overview of the AI job market.
- Salary & Skills Analysis – Detailed insights into salaries, skills, tools, and company size distribution.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze global AI job postings.
- Identify industries with the highest hiring demand.
- Compare AI job opportunities across countries.
- Explore salary trends for different AI job roles.
- Discover the most in-demand AI skills and tools.
- Provide an interactive dashboard for business decision-making.

## 📈 Key Insights

- The Automotive industry recorded the highest number of AI job postings.
- AI job opportunities are distributed across multiple countries.
- Data Analyst, NLP Engineer, AI Researcher, and Machine Learning Engineer are among the highest-paying AI roles.
- TensorFlow, LangChain, and other AI tools are frequently preferred by employers.
- Medium and large companies contribute significantly to AI hiring.
